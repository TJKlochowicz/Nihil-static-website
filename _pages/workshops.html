The NihiL Workshop

When & Where

31 January - 2 February 2024

Room 0.01, Bushuis, Kloveniersburgwal 48, Amsterdam

Zoom link: https://uva-live.zoom.us/j/87618965843

The workshop dinner will be held at Indrapura

Description

The goal of the workshop is to bring together linguists, philosophers, logicians, and cognitive scientists who share an interest in the interfaces between (non-classical) logic, language and cognition.

Registration

Registration is not required, but to ensure a smooth organization of the workshop, please register here! If you plan to attend online, please register to receive the zoom link.

Jeroen Groenendijk Memorial

On Thursday 1 February from 17.00-19.00 there will be an event commemorating Jeroen Groenendijk, who suddenly passed away on the 17th of October 2023. Jeroen has made groundbreaking contributions to formal semantics, including inspiring some of the core ideas behind the Nihil project. More information on this event will be posted here
