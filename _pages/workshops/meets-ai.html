The NihiL Meets AI Workshop

When & Where

06 February 2025 2024

SP107 Room F1.15,

Description

The goal of this event is to explore possible applications of the results of the Nihil project in AI and related areas. In the first part project members will present some of the results of the Nihil project and pitch ideas for possible future AI related applications. In the second part the AI advisers will give their feedback with suggestions and recommendations.
