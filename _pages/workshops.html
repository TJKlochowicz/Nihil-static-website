The NihiL Workshop

The First NihiL Workshop will take place on 31.01-02.02 2024.

The goal of the workshop is to bring together linguists, philosophers, logicians, and cognitive scientists who share an interest in the interfaces between (non-classical) logic, language and cognition.

Invited speakers: Oliver Bott (Bielefeld); Bart Geurts (Nijmegen); Marianna Girlando (ILLC); Hannes Leitgeb (Munich); Andreea Nicolai (ZAS Berlin); Salvador Mascarenas (ENS Paris) [TBC]; Greg Restall (St. Andrews) [TBC]; Fabian Schlotterbeck (Tubingen); Yasu Sudo (UCL); Jialiang Yan (Tsinghua); Fan Yang (Utrecht).
